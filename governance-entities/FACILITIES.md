# Facilities

## Abbreviations
AF = Adopting Facility
CF = Contributing Facility

## Definition
A facility is any entity, institute, group of people that has an active interest in the Project.
An _adopting facility_ is a facility that has installed SciCat in production and SciCat is part their technical infrastrucutre and data management.
A _contributing facility_ is a facility that has allocated at least half of an FT to actively contributing to the Project in any form or shape

## Responsibilities
There are no official responsibilities for an adopting facility, although, at times, they are called to provide feedback to the project.
A contributing facility has the responsibility to allocate resources equivalent to a half of an FT to sustain and contribute to the Project.
