# Advisory groups

## Abbreviation
AG = Advisory Groups

## Description
Advisory Groups (AG) are temporary groups of domain experts called to provide expert feedback on specific issues. 
Advisors should be individuals with the proper knowledge and not involved with the Project.

## Responsibilities
AG have the following responsibilities:
- listen carefully to the issue/topic they have been called to advise on
- provide requested feedback in the best suitable format

## Management
AG can be formed by the Project Leaders (PL) or Technical Leaders (TL) upon official request by the Contributors Community (CC) or as they see fit in managing the Project or the effort that they are leading.

## Duration
An Advisory Group should be limited in time and it ends once they have submitted the requested feedback and their recommendations.

## Output
The feedback from an AG should be recorded in written format and be available to the community.
It is responsibility of the individuals which requested advice of the AG to make sure the feedback is recorded and publicly available.
